Ladies...

and gentlemen.

*[pause]*

May the seventeenth. One o'clock. Seventy Scituate Street Arlington. The rear garden. Bring something.

*[pause]*

Jeannine will be at the grill.

*[longer pause]*

Now.

I could tell you that this is just a barbecue.

An ordinary —

*[pause]*

— barbecue.

On an ordinary street.

In an ordinary town.

On an ordinary Sunday.

In an ordinary —

*[pause]*

— May.

*[looks up]*

But we both know.

Don't we.

We both know.
